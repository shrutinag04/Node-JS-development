Senorita

by shawn mendes

Are you interested?

Details

Shawn Mendes of Canada and Camila Cabello of Cuba collaborated on the song "Seorita." It was released as the second single (seventh overall) from Mendes' self-titled third studio album's deluxe version on June 21, 2019, and was later included on Cabello's second studio album Romance (2019).

Mendes, Cabello, Charli XCX, Ali Tamposi, Jack Patterson, and the song's producers Andrew Watt, Benny Blanco, and Cashmere Cat wrote the song.
